About Me

Hey there!

I'm Khoa, a senior at Cal majoring in Computer Science, with expected graduation date this December. I also like Math, Stats, Econ, and Industrial Engineering, and have taken some classes in these fields as my electives. I love to build stuff, learn something new, and git push on a regular basis.

I'm most interested in Natural Language Processing, Data Mining, and their applications in the real world. I got my feet wet in NLP from my internship at Ocean Tomo, LLC in Summer 2013 working on the Patents Rating System, and is dabbling in ML through college classes and side projects. Currently I'm a research apprentice working with Professor Heather Haveman on mining information from the Bitcoin and vintage wine market data.

This summer, I will be a Distributed Computing/Data Science Intern at Autodesk! In the fall, I plan to continue/wrap up my research commitment, TA for EECS70: Discrete Math and Probability Theory, as well as look for full-time opportunities. It's gonna be a busy semester, but I'm sure it will also be very fun and memorable!

I recently develop an interest in Financial Engineering, and it leaves me in awe after taking my first graduate-level FE class because it is a mix of CS, Math/Stats, and Financial Economics -- all three of my most favorite subjects! Hopefully I'll get the opportunity to pursue an MFE or an MBA somewhere down the road, but we'll see.

Just shoot me an email at khoatran (at) berkeley (dot) edu if you wanna chat! D:

In my free time, I play tennis, racquetball, lift (or at least try to), watch English soccer, and read YA novels (somehow I have a strong obsession with dystopian stories).

Enjoy your stay on my webpage!
