About Me

Hey there!

I'm Khoa, a senior at Cal majoring in Computer Science. I also like Math, Stats, Econ, and Industrial Engineering, and have taken some classes in these fields as my electives. I love to build stuff, learn something new, and git push on a regular basis.

I'm most interested in Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and their applications in the real world. I got my feet wet in NLP from my internship at Ocean Tomo, LLC in Summer 2013 working on the Patents Rating System, and is dabbling in ML through college classes and side projects. Currently I'm a research apprentice working with Professor Heather Haveman on mining information from the Bitcoin and vintage wine market data, and also a reader for CS70: Discrete Math and Probability Theory, supervised by Professor Anant Sahai.

I'm also very interested in teaching, though I don't plan to go to grad school anytime soon. Before I graduate :(, I'm hoping to TA for a class at Berkeley and share my passion for Computer Science with other students.

If you want to chat, shoot me an email at khoatran (at) berkeley (dot) edu! D:

In my free time, I play tennis, racquetball, lift (or at least try to), watch English soccer, and read YA novels (somehow I have a strong obsession with dystopian stories).

Enjoy your stay on my webpage!
